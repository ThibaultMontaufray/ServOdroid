GO-4 is the secondary antagonist in WALL-E. He is a minion under Auto. Gopher has the ability to grab objects with a small tractor beam located in his "head" region. GO-4 is charged with getting rid of the plant WALL-E brings aboard; this includes his attempt at shooting it into space via space pod. GO-4 also assists Auto in mutiny against the Captain. When the Captain fights Auto GO-4 attempts to assist him but the Captain accidentally kicks him causing him to fall out a window where he lands on the cement and breaks.
